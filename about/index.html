Mikel Matticoli

(he/him/his)

I am an engineer, designer, and producer with a passion for making things and helping others. I’ve had the opportunity to work on teams and projects of all kinds – bridging the communication gap between both technical and non-technical stakeholders, guiding teams and individuals through solving complex problems, and doing a lot of hands-on design, development, and troubleshooting work myself.

I enjoy making things of all kinds – from websites, software, and robots, to crafts and foods. I’ve had the privilege of participating in and coaching competitive robotics for over 8 years, as well as making time for creative projects and household hacks. I find joy in things created by others too – particularly food, games, and stories, especially when they provide shared experiences with friends and family.
